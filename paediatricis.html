PAEDIATRICS & NEONATOLOGY

  • Our paediatrician is extremely skilled and experienced in providing medical care with a holistic approach
  • They offer comprehensive care of the most superior quality to all patients up to the age of 12 with immunisation facility
  • Neonatology unit at Praja Hospitals provides cloistered protective environment for the care of pre term low birth weight new born babies with sophisticated equipments with latest technology
